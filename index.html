Locasto Glinda Tattypoo

(a.k.a The Good Witch)

Motto: There's no place like home.

Tell me your troubles. If I cannot help you, I will find someone who can.

Who I am

I have ruled over and protected this county ever since the overthrow of the Wicked Witch of the South. I live in a palace near the southern border of the Quadling Country, attended by fifty beautiful maidens from each country of Oz. I am fiercely protective of my subjects in the South.

I have demonstrated my social consciousness with the construction of a gated communities for the rabbits of Bunnybury and the paper dolls of Miss Cuttenclip, as well as showing a personal interest in the concerns of not only the humanoid Quadlings, but also the other inhabitants of my jurisdiction. I am experienced and seasoned to the point of knowing that there is not a magic cure for everything, and that certain things cannot be changed or perhaps should not be changed for better or for worse.

Career Highlights

  • Official Ruler of Quadling County
  • Owner of the Great Book of Records
  • Advocate and protector of the Munchkins
  • CEO of the Female Soldier's Army
  • Instrumental in shaping the future of the Land of Oz as a whole
